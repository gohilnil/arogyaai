Heart Attack
Chest pain, arm pain, sweating
▼
1
Call 108 immediately — do not wait for symptoms to pass
2
Help them sit or lie; loosen tight clothing (collar, belt)
3
If conscious & not allergic: chew 1 aspirin (300–325mg) — NOT swallow whole
4
If unresponsive and not breathing: CPR — 30 compressions + 2 breaths, repeat
🚑 Every minute matters. Call 108 first!
🧠
Stroke — FAST Test
Facial droop, arm weakness, slurred speech
▼
F
Face: Ask to smile — one side drooping?
A
Arms: Raise both — one drifts down?
S
Speech: Repeat a phrase — slurred or confused?
T
Time: Call 108 immediately. Note when symptoms started.
🧠 Treatment must begin within 3–4.5 hours of onset!
🫁
Choking
Can't breathe, hands on throat, turning blue
▼
1
If they can cough, encourage strong coughing
2
Lean forward — 5 firm back blows between shoulder blades
3
Heimlich: stand behind, fist above navel, pull sharply inward & upward
4
Alternate 5 back blows + 5 abdominal thrusts until cleared or help arrives
🤕
Severe Bleeding
Heavy uncontrolled blood loss
▼
1
Apply firm direct pressure with clean cloth — do NOT remove
2
Elevate injured limb above heart level if possible
3
Do NOT remove embedded objects — stabilise them in place
4
Apply tourniquet 5–7cm above wound only if bleeding persists after 10 min
🔥
Burns
Scalding, thermal, chemical burns
▼
1
Cool with lukewarm (NOT cold) running water for 20 minutes
2
Do NOT apply ice, butter, toothpaste or oil
3
Cover loosely with clean cling film or sterile material
4
Call 108 for large, deep, facial or chemical burns
🍬
Diabetic Emergency
Confusion, sweating, shaking, collapse
▼
1
If conscious: give 15g fast sugar — 3 glucose tabs, 150ml juice, or 3 sugar cubes
2
Wait 15 min. Recheck. Repeat if still symptomatic.
3
If unconscious: call 108, recovery position, do NOT give anything by mouth
⚠️ Sugar >400 with vomiting: call 108 immediately
🤧
Severe Allergic Reaction
Swollen throat, hives, can't breathe
▼
1
Call 108 immediately — anaphylaxis closes the airway in minutes
2
If they have EpiPen: inject on outer thigh NOW, through clothing if needed
3
Sit upright for breathing difficulty; lay flat if BP is low
☠️
Suspected Poisoning
Swallowed chemicals, overdose, snake bite
▼
1
Call 108 immediately. Tell them what was ingested and when.
2
Do NOT induce vomiting unless specifically instructed
3
Snake bite: immobilize limb, keep below heart, do NOT suck venom
4
Poison India helpline: 1800-116-117
