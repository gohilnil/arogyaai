💡 Food Safety Tips
Fruits & Vegetables: Wash thoroughly before eating. Avoid bruised or discolored areas.

Cooked Food: Store at or below 5°C within 2 hours of cooking.

Packaged Items: Always check MFG/expiry dates and store per instructions.
⚠️ Note: AI analysis is based on visual cues. For packaged foods, always check printed expiry dates. When in doubt, discard.
